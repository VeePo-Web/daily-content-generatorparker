## A) HOW TO IDENTIFY THE RIGHT NANO PHOTOGRAPHERS

Sweet spot: 2,000–15,000 Instagram followers. Aspirational. Not yet fully premium. Aesthetics-first.

**Signals in their bio:**
- Niche-specific description: "destination wedding photographer," "luxury brand photographer," "travel + lifestyle"
- Bio links to nothing, a Linktree, or an outdated Squarespace site
- Language like "available for travel" or "worldwide" — destination ambition, local reach
- Personal email in bio (gmail, icloud) — no professional email/website system yet

**Signals in their captions:**
- Thoughtful about craft and aesthetic — thinking about their brand even if they haven't built one
- Occasionally posts about the business side: rate anxiety, client interactions, the freelance hustle
- Hashtags in 10K–500K range — intentional niche tags, not viral mass hashtags

**Their current site situation:**
- Google their name — find nothing, a generic Squarespace, or a photography directory listing
- Click their bio link — loads slowly, looks generic, last update 18+ months ago
- No custom domain — clearest indicator they haven't invested in digital presence

**The emotional qualifier:**
Their work should stop you mid-scroll. If their photos would stop a boutique hotel creative director, they qualify. These are the photographers who will be genuinely grateful for a gifted build.
